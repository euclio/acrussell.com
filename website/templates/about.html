About Me

My name is Andy Russell. I am a junior at Pomona College in beautiful Claremont, California.

I'm a programmer at heart, but I have significant experience in web design. I created this site my freshman year of college to improve my knowledge of both.

I've been programming for about 6 years. I'm comfortable with Java, Python, C++ and C. I can code HTML, CSS, and Javascript as well.

I've used UNIX plenty, and vim is my editor of choice! When I look at a problem, I try to survey it from every angle: I want to ensure that it is solved the right way the first time. I'm always striving to learn new skills and improve my current techniques. I also enjoy teaching others how to program.

In 2011 I graduated from Parkway West High School. At Pomona I expect to ear a B.A. in Computer Science.

I grew up just outside of St. Louis, Missouri. I love the outdoors, meeting people and playing video games.

I'd like to give a shoutout to my wonderful family, the spibs of H2E, and my cute dog Sadie!

Thanks for visiting my site! It's been a blast making it.
